Kosten solutions

Each solution is built to your needs, and so is the team that delivers them. We have a Service Delivery Leader (SDL) for you and your team, drawing on a right-shoring service approach.

Right-shoring refers to the strategic decision of a business or organization to select the most suitable combination for outsourcing its operations. The term is derived from the idea of finding the right mix of geographical locations to achieve the optimal balance of cost, quality, and proximity.
Kosten right-shoring aims to provide the right solution with the right tech team at the right price. Our approach provides the following benefits:

  • Cost advantages
  • Tech skill availability
  • Proficiency in English
  • Similar time-zone
  • Western culture

About us

Kosten aike roughly translates to “the place of the winds” in the traditional Tehuelche language of Patagonia. These winds are famous for sweeping through the mountain ranges, plateaus, hills, and ravines through to the water with a powerful energy that keeps Patagonia abundant and diverse today.
