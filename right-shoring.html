Right-shoring refers to the strategic decision of a business or organization to select the most suitable combination for outsourcing its operations. The term is derived from the idea of finding the right mix of geographical locations to achieve the optimal balance of cost, quality, and proximity. Kosten right-shoring aims to provide the right solution with the right tech team at the right price. Our approach provides the following benefits:

  • Cost advantages
  • Tech skill availability
  • Proficiency in English
  • Similar time-zone
  • Western culture

Design

A Proof of Concept or POC is a small-scale project designed to validate the technical feasibility of a particular idea, concept, or approach. The primary purpose of our POC is to demonstrate that a certain solution can be implemented effectively and to test its viability before the client commit to a full-scale development effort.

A Minimum Viable Product or MVP is a version of a new digital product that includes only the essential features needed to meet the core requirements of early adopters and gather feedback for future development. Our MVPs are designed to quickly bring a digital product to market with minimal development effort.
